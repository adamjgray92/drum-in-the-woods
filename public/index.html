Take advantage of the summer

The Drum in the Woods camp is a new event of its kind. It's a spectecular event for all ages that seeks to bring together drummers, dancers, and musicians of all background to gather and share their skills with the help from professional facilitators from around the world. For three days a number of events will take place and on the 23rd of July Saturday, A special concert (DRUMFEST) will be held at a venue in Sheffield to be confirmed soon.

Our Activities

Dance

You will learn world dances from experinced indviduals. dances from Africa,Brazil,Asia and the rest of the world.

Drumming

An exciting part of the camp, African drumming,percussion and all types of drums will be taught.

Music

You will learn most of world musical instruments i.e Marimba,Kora and many other instruments.

Film Making

Our staff are professionals trained in film and media will also run film-making workshops as part of the camp. It involves basics camera setting, shooting a film, and edit the final film. This is to make sure we cover all range of activities.
