Workshops

We offer primary, secondary schools, educational instututes, and disabled workshops with some of our experienced leading professionals in the educatuon and workshops field. Our workshops are well fully interactive to students. The workshops are designed in a way that it entertains while it educates we call it edutainment.

Our team has delivered workshops and perfomances in the following countries Canada, Brazil, Australia, African countries, Malaysia, New Zealand, Japan, France, Belgium, Hong Kong and United Kingdom.
